Project RangDe

Projects Project RangDe

Rang De, an inspiring initiative undertaken by the Rotaract District Organization RID 3012, aspires to imbue the world with boundless joy. With the tagline, "Rang De is Jahan Ko Khushiyon Se" (Paint the world with joy), this project embarks on a noble mission to revitalize the lusterless environs of NGOs, social service organizations, old age homes, orphanages, low-funded hospitals, animal care shelters, displaying an unwavering commitment to all sentient beings, and other deserving institutions. The primary objective is to adorn their walls with educational and inspirational doodles and paintings, thus fostering an ambiance of upliftment. In the previous tenure, Rang De exemplified the essence of volunteerism, with 800 passionate volunteers dedicating over 360 man-hours to embellish 200 walls across 45 venues. These remarkable efforts have profoundly impacted over 60,000 individuals, leaving a lasting impression of inspiration and positivity. The influence of Rang De transcends the boundaries of Delhi and the National Capital Region (NCR), steadily venturing forth through a befitting venture aptly named "Destination Rang De." By reaching out to diverse locations, this endeavor ardently endeavors to disseminate joy and inspiration across different states. Beyond the mere act of painting, Rang De places profound emphasis on fostering fellowship and friendship. Volunteers who actively participate in this noble cause forge deep bonds amongst themselves, nurturing a profound sense of camaraderie and shared purpose.
