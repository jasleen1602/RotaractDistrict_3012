Project AAINA

Projects Project AAINA

Through its engaging and informative sessions, Aaina creates a safe and inclusive space for young individuals to learn and discuss sensitive topics. The project's dedicated team of speakers ensures that the information is presented in an age-appropriate manner, taking into account the diverse backgrounds and experiences of the participants. By fostering an environment free of judgment, Aaina encourages open dialogue and encourages teenagers to ask questions, seek guidance, and share their concerns.

The monthly sessions offered by Aaina cover a range of important subjects. They address the physical and emotional changes associated with puberty, promoting an understanding and acceptance of these transformations. Consent, a fundamental aspect of healthy relationships, is explored to empower young individuals to establish boundaries and recognize the importance of mutual agreement. Additionally, the project addresses topics such as gender identity, sexual orientation, safe sex practices, contraception, and sexually transmitted infections, equipping teenagers with essential knowledge for a healthy and responsible approach to their sexual lives.

Recognizing that interactive learning enhances engagement and understanding, Aaina incorporates fun activities into its sessions. These activities may include group discussions, role-playing scenarios, educational games, and creative projects. By blending education with enjoyment, Aaina ensures that participants not only gain knowledge but also develop positive attitudes towards sexual health.

Overall, the Aaina project stands as a dedicated initiative to empower young minds with comprehensive sex knowledge. Through its informative sessions and interactive activities, Aaina strives to create a generation of informed, confident, and responsible individuals who can navigate their sexual lives with knowledge and respect.

Shreya Shukla - Project Chair
